Rules

Houston Rule


You can only trade picks within the same round, and you can only trade draft picks for other draft picks. You can only trade three picks per trade and you can only trade draft picks with any given team twice.


Broncos Rule


You can only trade players of the same position (to make sure that every team has every position for every game).


Pederson Rule


You may not blatantly tank in any game.

Defensive Pressure


If the DE or LB blitzes the QB, he cannot tackle the running back, but he can pressure or sack the QB.


Field Goals


The chance of the kicker making a field goal depends on whether the field goal is over or under 40 yards. Kickers have a constant chance of making any kick from game to game. The max distance of a kicker is 62 yards (the opponent's 45-yard line).


Punts


The kick will punt according to his distance distribution. If the punt goes into the end zone, the punter has a chance (listed as "aware" in stats) to move the ball somewhere inside the 20-yard line.


Fourth Downs


If the game is not in its final 10 minutes, teams will not go for it on fourth down. Otherwise, teams will go for it if they are inside of the opponent's 3-yard line. Teams will not go for it in any of the following circumstances:
- If the team is in field goal range and the team can win/tie with a field goal
- If the team is outside of field goal range and is ahead
- If a team is ahead and has over 2 yard to go
- If a team is behind by one possession inside its own 40 with over 3 minutes
If none of the above criteria are met, the decision is made by the following equation:
100 * (seconds_remaining / 60) + 25 * score_differential + 9 * distance_to_opponents_goal_line < 750
If the above equation is true, the team will go for it. Otherwise, the team won't go for it.

2-Point Conversions


All teams have a 50% chance of making a two-point conversion, regardless of players. Teams will go for a two-point conversion if the game is within the final 10 minutes and the point differential after the TD is -15,-13,-11,-10,-8,-5,-2,1,5 or 12.
